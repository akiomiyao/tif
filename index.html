Transopson Insertion Finder (TIF) - An efficient detector for insertions of transposable element from NGS data

TIF detectes transposition of transpososable elements from sequence data of NGS.

Detection of Tos17 transposition in japonica rice Nipponbare

Tos17 is an endogenous retrotransposon of rice. Tos17 is active only in cultured cells (Hirochika et al. 1996). Taking advantage of this property, we created 50,000 lines of mutant panels from rice individuals regenerated from cultured cells (Miyao et al. 2003). Insertion sites of Tos17 is determined by the TAIL-PCR method and Suppression PCR method, and is published in the mutant panel database. Tos17 insertion mutant lines have been distributed to researchers around the world and is useful for functional analysis of genes.
