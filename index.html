Transopson Insertion Finder (TIF) - An efficient detector for insertions of transposable element from NGS data

TIF detectes transposition of transpososable elements from sequence data of NGS.

Detection of Tos17 transposition in japonica rice Nipponbare

Tos17 is an endogenous retrotransposon of rice.
